How it works

Three steps. Nothing leaves your device.

  1. 1

    Drop a file

    Any image or PDF. It's opened locally in your browser — never uploaded.

  2. 2

    Review what's found

    Faces, emails, phones, cards & names are boxed. Hidden metadata, GPS and embedded threats are listed too.

  3. 3

    Download clean

    Pixels are destroyed and re-encoded with zero metadata — and Scrub verifies it for you.
